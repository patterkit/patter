Patter and its tools are open source and MIT-licensed. They also bundle a few third-party
components that keep their own licenses; the notable ones are collected here.

## Spell-check dictionaries and engine

The built-in English dictionaries come from [SCOWL / the English Speller
Database](https://wordlist.aspell.net/), under a permissive BSD/MIT-style license (attribution
only). The spell-checking engine is [`nspell`](https://github.com/wooorm/nspell) (MIT). Any
Hunspell dictionaries you import yourself keep their own licenses; that's between you and
wherever you got them. See [Spell-check](/spell-check/) for how these are used.
